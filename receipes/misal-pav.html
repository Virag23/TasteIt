Misal Pav

A fiery sprouted bean curry topped with crunchy farsan.

Prep:

15 mins

Cook:

20 mins

Serves:

1 People

Nutrition Facts

Calories 289 kcal
Protein 12g
Carbohydrates 42g
Fiber 8g
Fat 9g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Matki (Sprouted Moth Beans)
  • 2 Onions & 2 Tomatoes (Finely Chopped)
  • Misal Masala (The Secret Ingredient)
  • 1 cup Farsan (Crunchy Topping)
  • Fresh Lemon & Chopped Cilantro

How to Cook

The Base

Saut√© onions and spices until the oil separates from the masala.

Add the sprouted matki and water, then pressure cook for 2 whistles.

The Assembly

Pour the thin spicy gravy (rassa) into a bowl.

Top with a generous amount of farsan, onions, and lemon juice.
