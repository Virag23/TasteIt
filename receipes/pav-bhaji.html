Pav Bhaji

Buttery, mashed vegetable curry served with golden toasted buns.

Prep:

20 mins

Cook:

30 mins

Serves:

2 People

Nutrition Facts

Calories 400 kcal
Protein 10g
Carbohydrates 55g
Fiber 7g
Fat (Butter) 18g

Ingredients

  • 3 medium Potatoes (Boiled & Mashed)
  • 1 cup Cauliflower & Green Peas (Boiled)
  • 2 Onions & 2 Tomatoes (Finely Chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Butter (Amul butter recommended)
  • 2 tbsp Pav Bhaji Masala
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Fresh Pav (Bread Rolls)

How to Cook

The Bhaji

Heat butter in a large pan, saut√© onions until translucent, then add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes until soft.

Add the boiled vegetables and mash them thoroughly using a masher. Mix in the Pav Bhaji masala, salt, and water. Simmer with a huge dollop of butter.

The Pav

Slit the pav from the center. Heat a tawa, add a little butter and a pinch of masala.

Toast the pav on both sides until golden brown. Serve hot with lemon wedges and chopped onions.
