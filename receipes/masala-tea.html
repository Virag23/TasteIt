Masala Tea

The authentic Indian Chai experience.

Prep:

5 mins

Cook:

10 mins

Serves:

2 Cups

Nutrition Facts

Calories 80 kcal
Sugar 4g
Protein 2g

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 cup Milk (Whole or Oat)
  • 2 tsp Black Tea Leaves
  • 3 tsp Sugar
  • 1-inch Ginger (Crushed)
  • 2 Cardamom pods
  • Sugar to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil water in a saucepan and add crushed ginger and cardamom.
  2. Add Sugar and let it steam till it gets dissolves
  3. Add tea leaves and let it simmer for 2 minutes until dark.
  4. Pour in the milk and bring to a rolling boil.
  5. Strain into a cup and enjoy your hot Masala Tea!

Chef's Note

Always use fresh ginger for the best aroma.

Over-boiling tea leaves can make the chai bitter!
