Paneer Butter Masala

A rich, creamy, and buttery tomato-based gravy with soft paneer cubes.

Prep:

15 mins

Cook:

25 mins

Serves:

3 People

Nutrition Facts

Calories 360 kcal
Protein 14g
Carbohydrates 12g
Fiber 2g
Fat (Creamy) 28g

Ingredients

  • 250g Paneer (Cubes)
  • 3 large Tomatoes (Pureed)
  • 2 tbsp Butter & 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Cream
  • 10-12 Cashews (Soaked & Pasted)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 tsp Kasuri Methi (Dried Fenugreek)
  • Whole Spices (Cinnamon, Cardamom, Cloves)

How to Cook

The Makhani Base

Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add whole spices followed by ginger-garlic paste. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil starts to separate.

Stir in the cashew paste and spices (turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala). Cook for another 3-4 minutes on low heat until creamy.

The Finish

Add water to adjust consistency, then gently stir in the paneer cubes. Simmer for 5 minutes so the paneer absorbs the flavors.

Finish with fresh cream, a dollop of butter, and crushed kasuri methi. Serve hot with butter naan or jeera rice.
