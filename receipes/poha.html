Kanda Poha

A light, healthy, and traditional Maharashtrian breakfast classic.

Prep:

10 mins

Cook:

10 mins

Serves:

2 People

Nutrition Facts

Calories 180 kcal
Protein 4g
Carbohydrates 35g
Fiber 3g
Iron 10% DV

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Poha (Thick Flattened Rice)
  • 1 large Onion (Finely Chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Peanuts (Roasted)
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds & Cumin
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • Curry Leaves & Green Chilies
  • Lemon Juice & Fresh Coriander

How to Cook

Preparation

Place poha in a colander and wash gently under running water. Do not soak. Sprinkle a pinch of salt and sugar, mix lightly, and set aside.

Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, cumin, peanuts, curry leaves, and green chilies. Sauté until peanuts turn golden brown.

Final Tempering

Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent. Stir in turmeric powder and salt.

Add the washed poha and mix gently to coat with spices. Cover and steam for 2 minutes on low heat. Garnish with lemon juice and coriander before serving.
